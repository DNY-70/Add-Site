360 Invisible Drone

What is 360 invisible drone technology

We believe that drones have many more uses that can be used to capture our world in captivating ways. That’s why we took our time constructing a drone that can navigate small spaces and capture with great detail its surroundings whilst remaining completely invisible within the footage.

How it works

Let us know what your project is, what you are hoping to accomplish and schedule an appointment for our drone project director to help you bring your vision to life. After capturing your media we can also help with editing or simply deliver your files in your preferred format to you. 360 drone footage is compatible with our virtual showcase platform and can be integrated to deliver an even more immersive experience to your prospects.

Why should I use this

Drone footage is most often used in 2D form and although there are companies out there that can deliver 360 aerial drone footage it is often distorted once a closer more detailed approach is needed. We have built the perfect drone to combat the proximity issue which allows us to get really close whilst maintaining quality and smoothness. 360 drone footage is very customizable allowing you to get creative with your project.
